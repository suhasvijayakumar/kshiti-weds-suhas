Applying for visa

You can apply for a tourist visa to India at least 30 days before your expected arrival, by completing the online application at: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/tvoa.html. Usually within 5 days, you receive an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) by e-mail.

Upon arrival at the airport in India, show this document in exchange for a visa stamp in your passport.
